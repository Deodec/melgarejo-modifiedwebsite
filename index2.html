Grade

A Grade (級きゅう Kyū?) refers to the power-scaling system that ranks jujutsu ranging from grades one to four, and special grade. It is used to categorize the strength of jujutsu sorcerers, cursed spirits, cursed objects, and cursed tools. Jujutsu High assigns their sorcerer's grades to assess their skill level ascending from Grade 4 to Grade 1. Students generally start at Grade 4 upon joining the school, but this depends on experience.

Ranks Following rankings are used to classify jujutsu sorcerers, cursed spirits, cursed tools and cursed objects in general:

Grade 3 (三さん級きゅう Sankyū?)

Semi-Grade 2 (準じゅん二に級きゅう Jun Nikyū?)

Grade 2 (二に級きゅう Nikyū?)

Grade 1 (一いっ級きゅう Ikkyū?)

Special Grade 1 (特とく別べつ一いっ級きゅう Tokubetsu Ikkyū?)

Special Grade (特とっ級きゅう Tokkyū?)
